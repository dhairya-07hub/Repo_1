Diploma in Graphic Designing – Course Syllabus

Semester 1: Foundations & Visual Concepts

  • Introduction to Graphic Design & Industry Overview
  • Elements & Principles of Design
  • Drawing & Sketching for Designers
  • Color Theory
  • Typography (Font pairing, readability, digital type)
  • Visual Composition & Layout
  • Graphic Design Software Basics – Photoshop, Illustrator
  • History of Art & Design
  • Practical Exercises & Design Projects

Semester 2: Digital Skills & Branding

  • Advanced Graphic Design Tools (CorelDraw, InDesign)
  • Vector & Raster Graphics
  • Logo & Branding Design
  • Image Editing & Manipulation
  • Packaging & Publication Design
  • Infographics & Digital Illustration
  • Print Media: Brochures, Posters, Flyers
  • Portfolio Development
  • Industry Project / Internship
